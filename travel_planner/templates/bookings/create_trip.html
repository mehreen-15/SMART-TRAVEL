Budget Planning

Consider these expenses when planning your budget:

  • Accommodation: 30-40% of budget
  • Transportation: 15-20% of budget
  • Food: 20-25% of budget
  • Activities: 10-15% of budget
  • Miscellaneous: 5-10% of budget
